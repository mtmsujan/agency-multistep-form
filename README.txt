== Description ==

AgencyForms is a form builder plugin for WordPress that . With its intuitive interface, you can design complex forms to collect user data, generate leads, or facilitate various interactions on your website.

== Installation ==
 
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
2. Configure the plugin settings by navigating to the AgencyForms settings page in the WordPress admin panel.
   - Locate the shortcode for the multistep form on the settings page and copy it for use on your site.
3. Customize the AgencyForms according to your preferences and save the settings.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

1. How do I create a multi-step form?
   To create a multi-step form, follow these steps:
   - Go to the imjol Multistep Form settings page.
   - Click on "Add New Form" to start creating a new form.
   - Configure your form steps, fields, and settings as needed.
   - Save your changes and insert the form shortcode into any post or page where you want the form to appear.

2. Can I customize the look and feel of my forms?
   Yes, imjol Multistep Form provides various customization options to design forms that match your website's branding. You can customize colors, fonts, and more from the plugin settings.

3. How can I view form submissions?
   Form submissions are stored in the WordPress database and can be viewed from the "Submissions" section of each form in the plugin settings. You can also enable email notifications to receive submission data directly to your inbox.

4. What should I do if I encounter issues during installation or setup?
   If you encounter any issues during installation or setup, please refer to our troubleshooting guide in the plugin documentation. You can also reach out to our support team for assistance.
